8. SHIPPING MODE ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Standard Class is the most used shipping mode.
Same Day shipping has the lowest order volume.

Insight: Customers prefer Standard Class likely due to lower cost.
Offering incentives for higher-margin shipping modes could improve
delivery experience and revenue.

----------------------------------------------------------------
9. KEY RECOMMENDATIONS FOR MANAGEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. REDUCE DISCOUNTS on Furniture category — especially Tables
   and Bookcases which are generating consistent losses.

2. INVEST MORE in Technology category — highest profit margin
   and strong sales performance.

3. FOCUS MARKETING on California and New York — these two states
   drive one-third of total revenue.

4. AUDIT CENTRAL REGION pricing strategy — sales are decent but
   profits are disproportionately low.

5. TARGET CONSUMER SEGMENT with loyalty programs — they represent
   50%+ of the customer base.

6. REVIEW HOME OFFICE SEGMENT — untapped growth potential given
   the rise of remote work.
